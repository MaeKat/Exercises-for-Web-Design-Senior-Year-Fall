The Magnificent Maya Blitch

Who is Maya?

Maya is my friend who I met in Mrs. Coco's class in 10th grade. We were both taking Algebra II and she was helplessly lost so I started helping her in class. Then she needed more help and we started hanging out after school. We quickly then became more invested in each other's social life and assimilated our two groups easily. Since then we've been through a lot of fun times

What defines Maya?

Social and Outgoing

Maya loves people. She honestly can't stop interacting with people or else I'm pretty sure she'd explode so this leads to some pretty fun experiences. Like how she somehow befriends random strangers on the streets. I mean all of the sleep overs are actually exhausting since She just has so much energy but they're super fun and it's just wonderful to hang out with both her and everyone else... and chocolate. She absolutely loves chocolate. Pretty much 95% of her friends are there for the absolutely ridiculous amount of chocolate she consumes but she doesn't care she just loves talking to other people.
Maya pretty much does whatever she wants to. She'll just fall on the floor just to make a scene and she doesn't even care what anyone thinks of her. Maya will say exactly what she thinks no matter who can hear her and she won't censor herself. She'll go and do something crazy and then come back with an insane story to tell. Maya is completely and utterly herself and it's awesome.
