<h2>All About Jelly Beans(and more!)</h2>

<p>
Jelly beans take six to ten days to make. There are two varieties -- <span class = "thick">traditional beans</span> and <span class = "thick">gourmet.</span>
The gourmet beans are typically softer and smaller. 
Gourmet, unlike traditional, have flavor in both the center and the shell.
</p>

<p class = "right">
Not everyone likes jelly beans. 
You have to have a certain taste for it.
 Some quite enhoy these special candies!
</p>

<hr/>

<p>For the most part a jelly bean contains: </p>

<ul class = "square">
	<li>Sugar</li>
	<li>Corn syrup</li>
	<li>Food starch</li>
</ul>


<p>The steps for jelly bean making include: </p>
<ol>
	<li>Cooking and chemistry</li>
	<li>Starch casting</li>
	<li>Panning</li>
	<li>Sugar coating</li>
	<li>Packaging</li>
</ol>
